 Heat 1 — The Battle Begins

  The curtain rises on Mello 2026 and what an opening salvo! Eight songs entered the arena, but only the strongest survive. With 8 voters casting their
  verdicts, every point mattered — and the margins were razor-thin.

  Ghost stormed to a commanding victory with Stanized, the only song to break 40 points and the clear crowd favourite. No debate, no drama — pure dominance.

  The real bloodbath was the fight for second. The Haunted and H.E.A.T both landed on exactly 40 points, forcing a tiebreaker. In the end, The Haunted's In Fire
   Reborn lived up to its name — reborn from the flames of a tie with two top marks to H.E.A.T's one. A heartbreaker for H.E.A.T, but the road isn't over yet.
